Different Levels

We can tackle the math section depending on your current score range.

Score Range: 400 - 600

If you're still scoring in this range, not to worry! We will brush up on our Algebra I/II skills, then dive into some geometry problems. The goal after a couple sessions is to begin scoring 650 or higher.

Score Range: 600 - 700

This is a good place to start! If you're scoring in this range, odds are you have a good grasp on the easy math problems already. We will begin to tackle harder topics like trigonometry and word problems.

Score Range: 700 - 800

Scoring in this range means that you are highly experienced with SAT Math. While there is not much more to improve upon, we can tackle the most difficult word problems.
